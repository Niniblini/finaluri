SYSTEM OF A DOWN BIOGRAPHY

Formed in Glendale, California in 1994, System of a Down is a heavy
rock band made up of Serj Tankian, Daron Malakian,
Shavo Odadjian and John Dolmayan. Three of the group’s
albums debuted atop the Billboard 200 (2001’s ‘Toxicity’ and
2005’s ‘Mesmerize’ and ‘Hypnotize’) before going on hiatus in 2006. Four years later,
the guys reunited, and would later release two new
singles in 2020: “Protect The Land” and “Genocidal Humanoidz,” which both charted
in the top 20 of the Hot Rock & Alternative Songs chart. Their 2005 song
“B.Y.O.B.” earned them a Grammy for best hard rock performance.
